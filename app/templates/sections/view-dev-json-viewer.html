Response Anatomy

        
A successful /calculate response bundles scalar results with visualization arrays:

        

            
Field groupContents

            
                
Scalarsprice, Greeks (delta, gamma, vega, theta, rho...), model metadata

                
Graphinggraph_data (payoff/value curves), distribution_data (terminal density)

                
Heatmapsheatmap_data/spots, spot_time_*, vol_time_* grids

                
Surfacesvol_surface_strike/delta, gatheral_* (total variance, SVI fit, arbitrage diagnostics)

            
        

        
The viewer truncates arrays longer than 12 elements for readability — counts are shown in place of the tail.
