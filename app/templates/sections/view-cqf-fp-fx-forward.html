Interest Rate Parity: 6-Month Verification

Neil Graham verifies the 6-Month outright rate by compounding spot amounts at their respective interest rates:

Step 1: Compounding Base Currency (EUR)
EUR 1.0000 compounded at 3.42% for 181 days:
1.0000 × (1 + 0.0342 × 181/360) = EUR 1.017195
Step 2: Compounding Variable Currency (USD)
USD 1.0375 compounded at 4.15% for 181 days:
1.0375 × (1 + 0.0415 × 181/360) = USD 1.059148
Step 3: Calculating Outright Rate
Ratio of Compounded amounts:
1.059148 / 1.017195 = 1.041244

General Parity Framework

The outright forward rate ($F$) is locked via no-arbitrage relationships:

$$ F = S \times \frac{1 + r_v \frac{n}{\text{BASIS}_v}}{1 + r_b \frac{n}{\text{BASIS}_b}} $$
  • $S$: base spot rate
  • $n$: number of days to settlement
  • $r_v, \text{BASIS}_v$: Variable currency rate & day basis (e.g. USD, 360)
  • $r_b, \text{BASIS}_b$: Base currency rate & day basis (e.g. EUR, 360)
