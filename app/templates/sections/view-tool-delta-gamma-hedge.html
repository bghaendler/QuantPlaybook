Delta-Gamma Dynamic Portfolio Hedging Simulator

Hold a short call, hedge it daily with stock — or neutralize gamma with a second option — and watch the hedging error distribution over simulated paths.

Setup

Hedging Error Distribution (final P&L of short call + hedge)

With realized = implied and frequent rebalancing, the distribution collapses around zero (replication works). Set realized ≠ implied to see the systematic gamma P&L $\frac{1}{2}\Gamma S^2(\sigma_r^2 - \sigma_i^2)dt$; reduce frequency to see discrete-hedging noise; add the gamma hedge to kill both.
