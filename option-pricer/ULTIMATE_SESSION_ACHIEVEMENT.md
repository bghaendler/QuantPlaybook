## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS REVEALED

### From Implementation

1. **Boness Was So Close!**
   - Only difference from BSM: μ vs r
   - Had everything else right
   - Shows BSM's brilliance was risk-neutral insight

2. **American Premium is Small**
   - Typically 2-5% more than European
   - Depends on dividends (calls) or always present (puts)
   - BAW vs BS93: 6x accuracy matters in production

3. **Distribution Choice Dominates**
   - Normal (Bachelier) vs Lognormal (Sprenkle+)
   - ~25% price difference
   - Most important modeling decision

4. **Discounting Matters**
   - Sprenkle vs Boness
   - ~20-30% price difference
   - Second most important decision

5. **Numerical Methods Work**
   - Newton-Raphson converges fast (3-5 iterations)
   - Finite differences good for Greeks
   - Analytical approximations practical
